    Remaining tickets

    Tips for Step 1: Event Details

    Boost your sales and event attendance by:

    • Making sure your event's timezone is correct
    • Grabbing attention with a compelling event image
    • Adding a clear event description with FAQs (like refund policy or dress code)
    • Promoting all your upcoming events in one place with your organizer profile page
    • Selling tickets to multiple days and times on a single event page

    Need more help? Check out our "Guide to Getting Started"

    Tips for Step 2: Create Tickets

    Whatever your goals, Eventbrite is flexible enough to help you hit them. Choose standard ticketing, or customize your ticket options and prices by:

    • Setting your venue capacity or using a waitlist to make sure you use your space wisely
    • Creating multiple ticket types, that can go on-sale at different times
    • Offering group discounts
    • Changing the price of a ticket even after sales have started
    • Fundraising and collecting donations
    • Offering reserved seating with your custom venue map

    Eventbrite is 100% free to use if you're organizing a free event. For paid tickets, we charge a small fee, which you can pass on to attendees.

    Need more help? Check out our "Guide to Getting Started"

    Tips for Step 3: Additional Settings

    There are four levels of privacy for events that you can adjust at any time:

    • Public: Your event is visible on our site and search engines like Google. With public events, pick an event type and topic so attendees can find your event.
    • Private: Your event can't be found via search, but people with the URL can see the event page. (You can choose to let the event be shared on social media.)
    • Password protected: You set a password attendees must enter to see your event page.
    • Invite-only: Attendees can only register through the Eventbrite invitation you send them.

    Want to spread the word? Here are free tools you can use through Eventbrite:

    • Publish your event to Facebook
    • Send out email invitations
    • Customize your event page URL
    • Create and send discount codes
    • Sell tickets right on your website with Eventbrite widgets
    • Pull in tweets and photos onto your event page

    Need more help? Check out our Help Center for more information.
